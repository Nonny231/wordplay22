Gameplay

You can play by texting WOD to 20081 and accepting renewal. Once your subscription is confirmed, you'll receive word trivia questions. Follow the instructions — if you get the correct answer, you earn 10 points. Keep playing to increase your points and qualify to win either airtime or cash.

Yes! You can continue playing with our on-demand service by texting PLAY. This allows you to play up to 10 times per day at ₦50 per play, giving you more chances to earn points and win prizes.

Rewards & Prizes

Airtime rewards are instantly credited to the phone number you used to play. Once you qualify for the daily or monthly draw and win, your airtime will be sent directly to your account automatically.

An SMS instruction will be sent to you asking for your account details. Once received, your cash prize will be credited promptly to your account. Make sure to respond to the SMS within the specified timeframe to claim your reward.
